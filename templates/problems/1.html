After a few hours of tweaking, you are finally happy with how your character looks. It is now time to move on and choose a name! Since you are a seasoned RPG player, you have prepared a large list of names to choose from.

However, the rules that determine if a name is valid or not are very complex:

  • It must start with an upper case character
  • It cannot contain spaces
  • It cannot have three or more identical consecutive characters
  • If the name is not forbidden via the above rules, then it is valid

Here are a few example names:


  Aabcde (valid)
  2a     (invalid, does not start with uppercase character)
  He llo (invalid, contains a space)
  Abccc  (invalid, contains three consecutive 'c')

Given these rules, how many of the names on your list are valid?
